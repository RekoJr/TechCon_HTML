Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts determined to share innovative ideas and explore the future of computing. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential technology conferences worldwide, attracting experts, entrepreneurs, and learners from all sectors.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, promote collaboration, and empower individuals to shape the future through technology. We aim to create a space where ideas flourish and new technologies come to life.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Aisha Rahman

Dr. Rahman, a pioneer in artificial intelligence research, shared her groundbreaking work on ethical machine learning and its impact on global industries during TechCon 2021.

James Liu

James Liu, founder of RoboMind Technologies, discussed the future of human-robot collaboration and introduced cutting-edge robotic systems that are revolutionizing manufacturing.

Sofia Martinez

Sofia Martinez, a leading cybersecurity strategist, emphasized the importance of digital trust and resilience in the age of AI and data privacy during her TechCon 2022 keynote.
